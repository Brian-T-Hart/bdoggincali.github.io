My Favorite Animal

Basic Info

The Wombat is one of my favorite animals. I am not really sure why, other than the fact that it is super cute. To me, it looks like a cross between a koala bear and a hampster. It looks like a soft, cuddly critter. Every time I see one, I want to pet it.



Here are some basic facts

  • Wombats are quadrupedal (they walk on four legs)
  • Wombats are marsupials
  • Wombats have a backwards facing pouch
  • Wombats are native to Australia
  • Wombats dig extensive burrow systems
  • Wombats are herbivores
  • Wombats have sharp rodent-like front teeth and powerful claws
